Drift Notepad — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Short version: Drift Notepad does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything you create — notes, photos, categories, templates, and settings — stays on your device.

What data the app handles

Drift Notepad stores the following directly on your device, using standard local app storage (IndexedDB). None of it is sent anywhere:

  • Notes you write, including text formatting, checklists, and tables
  • Photos you attach to notes (accessed only when you choose one via your device's photo picker)
  • Categories, tags, and templates you create
  • App settings, such as your chosen theme and folder icons
  • A PIN, if you choose to set one, used only to lock individual notes on your own device

What the app does not do

  • No account or sign-in is required or offered
  • No data is sent to any server — Drift Notepad has no backend
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising libraries are included
  • No data is shared with third parties, because none is collected in the first place

Permissions the app requests

  • Photo/file access — used only when you actively choose a photo to attach to a note, via the system's own photo picker. The app never browses your files on its own.
  • Storage (Android 9 and earlier only) — used only to save an exported backup or CSV file to your device's Downloads folder when you tap Export.

Exporting and deleting your data

You are always in full control of your data:

  • The Export feature in Settings lets you save your notes, categories, templates, and settings as a backup file, and Save to Downloads or Share it wherever you choose.
  • Deleting a note moves it to Trash, where it is permanently removed after 30 days, or immediately if you empty the Trash yourself.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of its local data from your device.

Children's privacy

Because Drift Notepad does not collect any data from anyone, it does not knowingly collect data from children, and no additional handling is required.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same page with a revised "Last updated" date.
